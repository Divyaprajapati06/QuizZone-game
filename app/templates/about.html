About QuizZone

QuizZone is an online quiz platform specially designed for IT and Computer Science students. Our goal is to help learners test, practice, and improve their technical knowledge through interactive and well-structured quizzes.

Features

  • Subject-wise quizzes for IT & CS
  • Multiple-choice questions with instant feedback
  • Leaderboard to track progress and performance
  • Simple, clean, and responsive design

Our Mission

We aim to make technical learning engaging, competitive, and accessible for every student who wants to grow in the IT field.
